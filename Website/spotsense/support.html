About the App

SpotSense is a parking availability app that uses mmWave radar sensors to detect bay occupancy and display real-time spot availability to drivers. Sensor data is processed at the edge; no camera imagery is used.

Privacy Policy

SpotSense does not collect personal data beyond what is necessary for core functionality.

Location data is used solely to detect nearby available parking spots and provide navigation guidance.

Sensor data (mmWave radar) is processed entirely on-device at the edge. No images, video, or personally identifiable information is captured, transmitted, or stored.

We do not sell, share, or retain any user data for advertising or third-party purposes.
