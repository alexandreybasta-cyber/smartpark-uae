Privacy Policy

Effective date: 18 July 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how SpotSense handles information when you use our mobile application and related services.

Information We Collect

Location data — With your permission, we collect your device location to show nearby available parking spots, calculate walking distances, trigger geofence notifications, and hand off navigation coordinates to your chosen maps app (Waze, Apple Maps or Google Maps).

Saved places — You may save favourite destinations (such as "Work" or "Home"). These are stored locally on your device and are not uploaded to our servers.

Diagnostics — We may collect anonymised crash logs and performance diagnostics to improve stability. This data is not linked to your identity.

How We Use Information

  • Display real-time parking availability near you
  • Recommend optimal parking spots based on destination and saved places
  • Trigger geofence notifications when you approach your destination
  • Improve app performance and fix crashes

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent or share your personal information with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes. Navigation handoffs pass coordinates only to the maps app you explicitly choose.

Data Retention

Location data is processed in real time and is not retained. Saved places remain on your device until you delete them or uninstall the app.

Your Choices

You can manage location permissions at any time in your device settings. If you deny location access, core features such as nearby spot detection and geofence notifications will not be available.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this policy, contact us at support@spotsense.app.
