The Problem

30% of urban traffic is just
people looking for parking.

In Dubai and Abu Dhabi, drivers spend an average of 20 minutes circling for spots — burning fuel, increasing emissions, and losing time. Existing solutions rely on expensive cameras and wired sensors that need constant maintenance.

Wired Sensors Fail

Traditional ultrasonic and magnetic sensors need cables, batteries, and frequent maintenance. Harsh UAE heat accelerates battery degradation.

No Real-Time Intelligence

Most parking systems show static data. No predictive guidance, no zone coordination, no integration with navigation apps like Parkin or RTA.

Malls Are Blind Spots

Indoor parking garages across Dubai Mall, MOE, and Yas Mall have zero visibility. Thousands of spots, no real-time data, no guidance.

Agentic Intelligence

Four AI agents that think,
coordinate, and predict.

The agentic layer is where SmartPark becomes intelligent. Four specialized agents work together — on-device, at zone level, across the city, and in conversation with drivers.

Edge Sensor Agent

Runs on each light-powered device

Makes local occupancy decisions in <200ms. Filters noise from shadows and passing traffic. Sends compressed state updates to the zone coordinator only when status changes — minimizing bandwidth.

Zone Coordinator Agent

Aggregates block-level data

Aggregates data from all sensors in a zone. Balances traffic across rows to prevent congestion. Detects anomalies — a spot occupied for 12+ hours triggers an alert. Coordinates with neighboring zones to redistribute overflow.

Prediction Agent

Forecasts availability patterns

Learns from historical patterns, events, weather, and time of day. Predicts spot availability 30 minutes ahead with 94% accuracy. Flags peak hours and suggests alternative zones before congestion builds.

Driver Guidance Agent

Conversational navigation interface

Natural-language interface integrated with Parkin and RTA apps. Guides drivers to the nearest available spot, answers questions about availability, and proactively suggests alternatives when preferred zones fill up.

Technology Stack

Built on Alibaba Cloud.

SmartPark uses the full Alibaba Cloud AI and IoT stack — from device connectivity to Qwen-powered agentic intelligence.

☁️

IoT Platform

Device registration, OTA firmware, shadow state, and rule engine for sensor fleet management at city scale.

🧠

Qwen-Max + Qwen-VL

Powers the conversational AI assistant and vision-based verification. Qwen-Max handles NLU and planning; Qwen-VL validates sensor anomalies via camera feeds.

🗄️

PolarDB + AnalyticDB

PolarDB stores real-time sensor state and booking data. AnalyticDB (PostgreSQL + vector) enables predictive queries and historical pattern analysis.
